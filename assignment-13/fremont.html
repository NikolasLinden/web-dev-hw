The city of Seattle has many sections and districts within it's city limits. From the historical Pioneer Square to the University of Washington campus. However, there is one that stands out as a quirky yet unique suburb. Fremont is a small neighborhood of Seattle right underneath the George Washington Memorial Bridge, located South of Greenlake and at the mouth of the Lake Washington Ship Canal.
