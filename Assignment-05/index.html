How to make scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • pinch of Salt and Pepper
  • 1/4 cup milk

Directions

  1. Start Heating up the pan
  2. Whip milk, eggs, and salt and pepper together in a bowl
  3. Place butter in pan until it is liquid, then add in egg mixture
  4. Cook for until all egg liquid is gone
  5. Serve ASAP. The food does not keep long
