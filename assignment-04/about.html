Edgar Allan Poe

Edgar Allan Poe was a poet in the early 1800's, and is famous for many of his poems, including the one that linked you here, The Raven. His life was dark, his parents dying when he was three and his career as a poet starting off slow. His writings became increasily dark as time went on, and while his work did get noticed near the end of his life, his popularity grew after his death and he was recognized as one of the great poets of his time.
